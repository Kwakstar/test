System Notes

  • Wire the 0-5V converter output to Arduino Uno pin A0 with common ground.
  • Drive the relay input from Arduino pin D8 and switch the 24V valve circuit on the relay contacts.
  • The app assumes 0-5V equals 0-10 bar and the valve is normally closed.
